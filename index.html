Equip communities with the technology to co-create and collaborate.

The spec is two published papers: Dual-Ledger Authentication and Local Assembly.

Houston Bloom is a field program built on the Community Legibility Spec. It gives a community the tools to find what it needs in itself, build on each other's work, and own the data it makes together.
